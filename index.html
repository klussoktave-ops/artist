Philanthropy

IMPACT BEYOND PROFIT

Oktave believes true success is measured by legacy and upliftment.

His philanthropy includes co-directing A Boy Is BORN, a community-based organization established in 2015.

He has pioneered local initiatives across Kiambu County, including Read and Ride, a mobile bus library designed to build a reading culture.

Youth No Wings, a local project for ages 18 to 24, introduces young people to bike riding and skating, with recent expansion into kite skiing and paragliding programs in Watamu.

These programs focus on nurturing and harnessing youth potential, especially among young men in the community.

He is also actively involved in policy advocacy in Kiambu County, contributing to a bill focused on film tax rebates, AI, and authorship.

Impact is not charity.
It is responsibility.
